## Dictionaries

- [Electronic Dictionary of Old/Middle Irish](https://web.archive.org/web/20231023133029/http://edil.qub.ac.uk/dictionary/search.php)
  - Notes: The most important Irish dictionary. With quick and advanced searches--see the help link on the right side of the toolbar.

- [Old Irish Base Form Dictionary](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/)
  - Notes: Part of the great University of Texas collection.

- [English-Old Irish Dictionary Online](https://en.glosbe.com/en/sga)
  - Notes: Seems a bit small compared to other online options.

- [Glossary and Verbforms Database](https://www3.smo.uhi.ac.uk/sengoidelc/duil-belrai/english.html)
  - Notes: Different ways of searching, plus a thesaurus.

- [English-Early Irish Wordlist](https://web.archive.org/web/20231023133029/http://www.wales.ac.uk/Resources/Documents/Research/CelticLanguages/EnglishEarlyIrishWordList.pdf)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A PDF over 1000 pages long (!).

- [Dictionary of Old Irish Glosses](https://indogermanistik.univie.ac.at/)
  - Notes: For the Milan Codex Ambrosianus.

- [Dictionary with Compendious Grammar](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=lM5EAAAAcAAJ&pg=PT42&lpg=PT42&dq=old+irish+lexicon&source=bl&ots=H-IzYhijMu&sig=o2S8SZAzcC1hZ0iQd_DR7HSLgJw&hl=en&sa=X&ei=-UV0VPagLMWpNqf5gMgL&redir_esc=y)

- [Proto-Celtic Word List](https://www.scribd.com/doc/20623905/A-Checklist-of-Proto-Celtic-Lexical-Items)
  - Notes: For several different languages.
