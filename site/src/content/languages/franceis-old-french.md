## Dictionaries

- [The Anglo-Norman Dictionary](https://www.anglo-norman.net/gate/)
  - Notes: This is the best resource for Old French around.

- [Lexique d'ancien français](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401163733/http://people.ucalgary.ca/~dcwalker/Dictionary/dict.html#database)
  - Notes: The whole site is in French.

- [Old French Master Glossary](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/)
  - Notes: Linked to a bunch of different texts.

- [Old French Base Form Dictionary](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/)
  - Notes: Similar to the link above, but with truncated and more general forms.

- [English-Old French Glossary](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/)
  - Notes: A very long list of words.

- [Historical French Dictionaries](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401163733/https://artfl-project.uchicago.edu/content/dictionnaires-dautrefois)
  - Notes: Going back to the 1600's, viewable all on the same page.

- [A dictionary of the Norman or Old French language](https://archive.org/details/dictionaryofnorm00kelhiala)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: Pulled from a ton of different sources; viewable online or in a PDF.

- [A Dictionarie of the French and English Tongues](https://www.pbm.com/~lindahl/cotgrave/)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: From 1611--you can download the whole thing in a giant PDF too.

- [Old French Dictionary](https://www.godswatcher.com/dictionary.htm)
  - Notes: Organized by letter, as most dictionaries are.

- [English-Old French Dictionary](https://en.glosbe.com/en/fro)
  - Notes: You can change it to Old French-English by clicking on the left side.

## Texts

- [ARTFL-FRANTEXT](https://artfl-project.uchicago.edu/artfl-frantext)
