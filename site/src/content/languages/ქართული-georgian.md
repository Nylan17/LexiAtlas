## Dictionaries

- [Georgian Online Lexicon](https://lexicon.ge/)
  - Notes: For use with many different languages.

- [Margaliti Dictionary](https://dictionary.ge/)
  - Notes: You'll need to know a bit of Georgian to make good use of this.

- [Translate Georgian](https://www.translate.ge/)
  - Notes: A good online resource for translating Georgian.

- [German-Georgian Dictionary](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401170757/http://www.foreignword.com/dictionary/Georgian/)
  - Notes: Site instructions are in English, oddly.

- [English-Georgian Dictionary](https://www.georgianweb.com/)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: In a nice PDF file.

- [English-Georgian Online Dictionary](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401170757/http://www.kvali.com/dictionary/v1/index.asp)
  - Access: Font
  - Notes: Make sure you have a Georgian font installed for this one.

- [English-Georgian Wordlist](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401170757/http://czudovo.info/list.php?what=1&ln=ka&in=from_en)
  - Notes: Get ready to sort through some serious lists.

- [English-Georgian NPLG Dictionary](https://www.nplg.gov.ge/gwdict/index.php?a=list&d=1&p=1)
  - Notes: Easy to navigate and use.

## Grammars

- [The Georgian Language](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401164033/http://www.armazi.demon.co.uk/georgian/)
  - Notes: An outline of the grammar, with many different topics.

- [Analysis of Georgian Morphosyntax](https://arts-sciences.buffalo.edu/linguistics.html)
  - Notes: This is a thesis and will require some knowledge of linguistics.

- [A Georgian Reading Grammar](http://www.seelrc.org:8080/grammar/pdf/stand_alone_georgian.pdf)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A systematic approach to learning to read the language.

## Charts & Aids

- [Georgian Transliteration Tool](https://translit.cc/ge)
  - Notes: Useful for learning the alphabet.

- [The Unbound Bible](https://www.biola.edu/talbot/unbound)
  - Notes: Bible texts online in many languages with parallel viewing.
