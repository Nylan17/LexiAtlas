## Dictionaries

- [Frisian Dictionaries](https://web.archive.org/web/20231016102432/http://www.allezhop.de/frysk/wb.htm)
  - Format: TXT
  - Notes: Various text files, with various modern languages as well.

- [Frisian Lemma List](https://tdb.fryske-akademy.eu/tdb/index-en.html)
  - Notes: Click "search" on the left side to start; enter a word, and then click "Betsjutting" at the bottom for the meaning (in Dutch), or "Kwic" for a list of places that the word appears.

- [Glossary of Frisian-English](https://archive.org/stream/phonologygrammar00sipmuoft#page/134/mode/2up)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A part of an older book in PDF form.

- [West Frisian Phrasebook](https://wikitravel.org/en/West_Frisian_phrasebook)
  - Notes: For traveling back in time, and for going to places where they speak Frisian today.

- [West Frisian Wordlist](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:West_Frisian_Swadesh_list)
  - Notes: About 200 words.

- [Frisian Wörterbuch](https://archive.org/details/wrterbuchderos01dooruoft)
  - Notes: An older dictionary, written in German.

- [Ostfriesisches Wörterbuch](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=THFsAAAAIAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Also very old, and also in German.

- [An attempt at a Frisian-Dutch dictionary](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=50QSAAAAIAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Just an attempt, mind you! You'll need to know Dutch.

- [Glossarium der friesischen Sprache](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=4hIGAAAAQAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: Also written in German, a freely available PDF.

- [Altfriesischen Wörterbuch](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=qhMGAAAAQAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Might be even older, but still written in German.

## Grammars

- [West Frisian Grammar](https://web.archive.org/web/20231016103134/http://cryptm.org/~nort/linguistics/West%20Frisian.pdf)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: An older book, but covers many different topics.

- [Phonology and Grammar of West Frisian](https://archive.org/details/phonologygrammar00sipmuoft)
  - Notes: Another link to the same book above--probably the best bet for English speakers.

- [Grammar course in Frisian](https://web.archive.org/web/20231016103134/http://www.allezhop.de/frysk/)
  - Notes: Various lessons and vocabulary--probably shouldn't be your first ancient language, though. Choose the language you want from the middle section.

- [Taalportal Frisian Linguistics](https://www.taalportaal.org/taalportaal/topic/)
  - Notes: This site appears to be under active development--grammar topics on the left side.

- [Frisian grammar notes](https://web.archive.org/web/20231016103134/http://www.allezhop.de/frysk/grammeng.htm)
  - Notes: To accompany the lessons above; availalbe in other languages as well.

- [West Frisian Phonology](https://omniglot.com/writing/westfrisian.htm)
  - Notes: A useful set of tables and instructions.

- [West Frisian Grammar on Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Frisian_grammar)
  - Notes: A helpful introduction.

- [Grammar of North Frisian](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=At0FAAAAQAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Written in German as well.

- [Frisische Sprachlehre](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=7HnnMgL1p0sC&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Lessons in German, from a very old book.

- [Altfriesisches Lesebuch mit Grammatik und Glossar](https://www.ling.upenn.edu/~kurisuto/germanic/of_heuser_about.html)
  - Notes: In German, unfortunately.

- [West Frisian Number Rules](https://www.languagesandnumbers.com/how-to-count-in-west-frisian/en/fry/)
  - Notes: For counting in Frisian!

## Texts

- [Integrated Frisian Language Database](https://tdb.fryske-akademy.eu/tdb/index-en.html)
  - Notes: Click "search" on the left side, then enter a word and click "Kwic" at the bottom of the pop-up. You'll then see a list of places where that word occurs.

- [Codex Unia](https://tdb.fryske-akademy.eu/tdb/index-unia-en.html)
  - Notes: Viewable in various parts.

- [Titus Frisian Corpus](https://titus.uni-frankfurt.de/texte/etcs/germ/afries/afrcorp/afrco.htm)
  - Notes: Select the text you want in the bottom right-hand window.

- [Written Frisian examples](https://www.heardutchhere.net/Frisian.html)
  - Notes: The Lord's prayer, and a few other things, with audio.

- [Altfriesisches Lesebuch](https://archive.org/details/altfriesischesle00heusuoft)
  - Notes: That means it's a Frisian reader!

- [Thet Freske Riim](https://books.google.fr/books?id=r2Q_AAAAcAAJ&printsec=frontcover&hl=fr#v=onepage&q&f=false)
  - Notes: A very old Frisian poem

- [Frisian Texts](https://books.google.fr/books?id=519SAAAAcAAJ&printsec=frontcover#v=onepage&q&f=false)
  - Notes: Not for the beginner, this one.

- [Bible in Frisian](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401170944/http://images.tresoar.nl/wumkes/pdf/Bibel_Wumkes_1943_watermerk.pdf)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: In a nice (modern) PDF file.

- [Gospel of Matthew in Frisian](https://archive.org/details/hetevangelievan00halbgoog)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: From a book in PDF form.

- [Twitter accounts in Frisian](https://indigenoustweets.com/fy/)
  - Notes: Useful for picking up the vernacular, I guess?
