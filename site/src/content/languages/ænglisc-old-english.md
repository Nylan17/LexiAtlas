## Texts

- [Anglo-Saxon Gospels](https://archive.org/details/gothicandanglos00ulfigoog)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A PDF download of about 22.2 MB.

- [Anglo-Saxon Individual Gospels](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=4BNAAAAAYAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=intitle:gospel+intitle:anglo-saxon&hl=en&sa=X&ei=PFjWT6fkOeji2AXc0aGoDw&redir_esc=y)

- [The Labyrinth](https://web.archive.org/web/20230604025411/http://labyrinth.georgetown.edu/)
  - Notes: Search by category and find primary texts written in the language.

- [Oxford Manuscripts](https://digital.bodleian.ox.ac.uk/)
  - Notes: Search this page for "old english" and "anglo-saxon" to see the offerings.

- [Old English Aerobics Anthology](https://www.oldenglishaerobics.net/)
  - Notes: Extremely useful--interactive readings and grammar helps to learn the language right.

- [Beowulf Online](https://www.humanities.mcmaster.ca/~beowulf/main.html)
  - Notes: Much better than that awful movie.

- [Old and Middle English Texts](https://www.hs-augsburg.de/~harsch/anglica/Chronology/e_chrono.html)
  - Notes: Divided by centuries.

- [Complete Corpus of Anglo-Saxon Poetry](https://web.archive.org/web/20230604025411/http://www.northvegr.org/old%20icelandic%20old%20english%20texts/selected%20corpus%20of%20anglo%20saxon%20poetry/index.html)
  - Notes: Many different items.

- [Aelfric's Homilies](https://users.ox.ac.uk/~stuart/kings/)
  - Notes: On Judith, Esther, and Maccabees. Includes grammar helps and a glossary. See also the "teaching texts" in the upper left, which provide some more commentary.

- [Eschatological Homilies](https://web.archive.org/web/20230604025411/http://webpages.ursinus.edu/jlionarons/wulfstan/wulfstan.html)
  - Notes: The link to the texts is on the left, called "Homilies." Be sure to view them as glossed (link is at the top after choosing a text) to get vocabulary help.

- [An Anglo-Saxon Reader](http://lexicon.ff.cuni.cz/texts/oe_bright_about.html)
  - Notes: From the Germanic lexicon page.

- [An Anglo-Saxon Reader PDF](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=z_ItAAAAYAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=intitle:anglo-saxon+intitle:reader&hl=en&sa=X&ei=KVfWT8GYHcHg2gWhhcyZDw&redir_esc=y)

## Other Resources
