## Grammars

- [Old Akkadian Writing and Grammar](https://web.archive.org/web/20230924080602/http://oi.uchicago.edu/pdf/mad2.pdf)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A textbook that is not that old. A very good resource.

- [Akkadian Linguistics](https://web.archive.org/web/20230924080602/http://128.97.6.202/EL2/Buccellati_1997_Akkadian_IN_The_Semitic_Languages.pdf)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: About 30 pages of text which cover may different grammar topics.

- [Akkadian Linguistics II](https://web.archive.org/web/20230924080602/http://www.sron.nl/~jheise/akkadian/akkadian.html)
  - Notes: Not fully comprehensive, but has explanations of many grammatical concepts and is easy to navigate.

- [Wikipedia's Grammar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akkadian#Grammar)
  - Notes: Very useful, and can be made into a different file format easily.

- [Languages Gulper](https://www.languagesgulper.com/eng/Akkadian.html)
  - Notes: Some introduction to the linguistics of Akkadian.

- [You Can Write in Akkadian](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JggfiiLTxMI&feature=channel&list=UL)
  - Format: Video
  - Notes: No you can't, actually, but you'll be able to write a few words at least after watching these videos.

- [Introductory course for Old Babylonian](https://web.archive.org/web/20230924080602/http://www.helsinki.fi/~whiting/intbab.html)
  - Notes: Some great resources online for learning this middle-period dialect.
