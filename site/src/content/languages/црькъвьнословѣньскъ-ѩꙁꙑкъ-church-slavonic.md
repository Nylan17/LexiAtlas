## Texts

- [Slavonic Bible Program](https://www.biblical-data.org/OCS/Slavonic_Bible.htm)
  - Notes: An excellent program for reading the OCS Bible, both the OT and the NT, plus the text in Greek, Latin, French, German, and others. You must enter a password to install the program. To get the password, visit [http://yegor.info/bible](http://yegor.info/bible) and email the proprietor. He will respond quickly with the password. Do not miss this program.

- [OCS New Testament](https://www.biblical-data.org/OCS/Slavic_NT.pdf)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A beautiful PDF file.

- [Church Slavonic Bible](https://www.wco.ru/)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: Viewable online and has downloadable PDF files as well. A great site.

- [Quattuor Evangeliorum Codex Glagoliticus](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=mK-JAAAAMAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=intitle:Quattuor+intitle:Evangeliorum+intitle:Codex+intitle:Glagoliticus&hl=en&sa=X&ei=vQXqT4HPK8iQ2AWwnf3pCA&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: The four gospels in OCS.

- [Quattuor Evangeliorum Codex Marianus](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=46gGAAAAQAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=intitle:Quattuor+intitle:Evangeliorum+intitle:Codex+intitle:Glagoliticus&hl=en&sa=X&ei=vQXqT4HPK8iQ2AWwnf3pCA&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: The four gospels from another manuscript.

- [Old Church Slavonic Electronic Corpus](https://web.archive.org/web/20221113182435/http://www.helsinki.fi/slaavilaiset/ccmh/)
  - Notes: The characater encoding is an issue here, but it's a great site with a number of different texts.

- [Old Church Slavonic Prayers](https://orthodoxepubsoc.org/prayers.htm)
  - Notes: Text plus downloadable MP3 files.

- [Codex Suprasliensis](https://web.archive.org/web/20221113182435/http://csup.ilit.bas.bg/node/1)
  - Notes: One of the oldest pieces of literature in the language.

- [Old Church Slavonic Textual Sources](https://kodeks.de/AKSL/AKSL.Quellen.htm)
  - Notes: Site is in German, but contains a great deal of useful information.

- [Old Slavonic Sample Manuscripts](https://www.biblical-data.org/OCS/Slavonic_samples.htm)
  - Notes: Pictures of different pieces.
