## Dictionaries

- [Gothic Bible Lexicon](https://www.wulfila.be/gothic/browse/)
  - Notes: Includes the text of the Bible; definitions are accessed by clicking words, but they are in German.

- [Meso-Gothic Glossary](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=VBsJAAAAQAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: An older book, but useful; PDF download is 6.0 MB.

- [Gothic/Greek/German Dictionary](https://www.wulfila.be/lib/streitberg/1910/)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: Many different formats--HTML, PDF, XML, etc. Part of the Wulfila project.

- [Online Gothic-English Dictionary](https://www.freelang.net/online/gothic.php)
  - Notes: Also has Gothic-English.

- [German-Gothic Dictionary](https://archive.org/details/deutschgotisches00prieuoft)
  - Notes: Written in 1890. Not surprisingly, many works on Gothic are in German.

- [Gothic Dictionary](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=TG8HAAAAQAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Again, written in German.

- [Comparative Glossary](http://archive.org/details/comparativegloss00balguoft)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A very large PDF file, but attention given to English.

- [Different Version, Same Glossary](http://lexicon.ff.cuni.cz/texts/goth_balg_about.html)
  - Notes: I guess if your name is Balg, you have to study Gothic.

- [Etymological Dictionary](https://archive.org/details/KurzgefasstesEtymologischesWrterbuchDerGotischenSprache)
  - Notes: Again, it will help to know German. As in, you'll need to know German in order to read this.

- [Glossary of the Gothic Language](https://archive.org/details/glossariumdergot00gabe)
  - Notes: German will also help here.

- [Comparative Dictionary of Gothic](https://archive.org/details/vergleichendesw01diefuoft)
  - Notes: Written in German.

- [Etymology of the Gothic Language](https://archive.org/details/grundrissdergoti00feis)
  - Notes: You guessed it, in German.

## Grammars

- [Gothic Grammar](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/)
  - Notes: Reference and lessons, coordinated with Bible passages; fairly comprehensive as well as technical in many parts.

- [Wright's Gothic Grammar](https://www.ling.upenn.edu/~kurisuto/germanic/goth_wright_about.html)
  - Notes: In several formats. Though somewhat inconvenient to navigate, it is worth checking out.

- [Gothic Grammar with Readings and Glossary](https://archive.org/details/gothicgrammarwit00brauuoft)
