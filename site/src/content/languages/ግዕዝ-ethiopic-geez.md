## Dictionaries

- [Lexicon Linguae Aethiopicae](https://archive.org/details/lexiconlinguaeae00dilluoft)
  - Notes: An Ethiopic-Latin dictionary. A very large download, but a good resource to have.

- [Dictionary of the Amharic Language](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=ngdHAAAAcAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=intitle:dictionary+intitle:amharic&hl=en&sa=X&ei=pZvHT95UkYnYBa21gYcL&redir_esc=y)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A single PDF.

- [Amharic Dictionary](http://www.amharicdictionary.com/)
  - Notes: Runs online; gives transliterations.

- [Another Amharic Dictionary](https://web.archive.org/web/20230924073510/http://www.ethiopiandictionary.com/)
  - Notes: Online as well, with transliterations.

## Grammars

- [Dillmann's Ethiopic Grammar](https://archive.org/details/EthiopicethiopianLanguageGrammarByAugustDillmann)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: Text-searchable; this download is a PDF of about 16 MB.

- [Grammatica Aethiopica](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=UIoBAAAAMAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=intitle:grammatica+intitle:aethiopica&hl=en&sa=X&ei=P5vHT-W-BIPC2wXHmejzCg&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: With paradigms, literature, and a chrestomathy.

- [Dillmann's Grammar in German](https://archive.org/details/grammatikdert00dill)
  - Notes: Published around the same time.

- [Grammaire &eacute;thiopienne](https://archive.org/details/grammairethiop00chauoft)
  - Notes: Ethiopic grammar written in French.

- [Classical Ge'ez Blog](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401164710/http://classicalethiopic.blogspot.com/)
  - Notes: A very useful website featuring explanations of many grammar principles. See the post list on the right of the site to navigate.

- [Amharic Survival Guide](https://fieldsupport.dliflc.edu/products/amharic/ac_bc_LSK/default.html)
  - Notes: In a variety of formats, including audio.

- [Amharic Basic Course](https://www.fsi-language-courses.org/fsi-amharic-basic-course/?page=Amharic)
  - Notes: From the Foreign Service Institute.

- [Video Songs and Basics](https://www.youtube.com/user/FHLProduction)
  - Format: Video
  - Notes: An entire Youtube channel.

- [Video for Alphabet](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BDoKd4zECOI&noredirect=1)
  - Format: Video
  - Notes: Hosted on Youtube. Check out the other videos on this channel as well.

- [Alphabet and Basics DVDs](https://fhlethiopia.com/index.html)
  - Notes: This site sells childrens' DVDs for learning the Amharic alphabet and some other basics. If you've tried these, let us know!

- [Alphabet info and chart](https://www.omniglot.com/writing/ethiopic.htm)
  - Notes: It seems like you can't have enough help trying to learn all the characters.

- [Alphabet chart](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401164710/http://ethiopia.limbo13.com/index.php/amharic_symbols/)
  - Notes: Another useful explanation with a small .gif file that you can easily download.

## Texts

- [Amharic Bible](https://www.wordproject.org/bibles/am/)
  - Notes: All laid out and ready to go.

- [Amharic Scriptures](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401173107/http://www.good-amharic-books.com/library?category=Scripture)
  - Notes: Lots of different texts, NT and others.

- [Ethiopic New Testament](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=Ljg2AAAAMAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: PDF is about 21.1 MB.

- [Ethiopic Old Testament PDF](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=v6fcT57q7qgC&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Appears to be the same texts as above, in various volumes. The link goes to volume one.

- [The Unbound Bible](https://www.biola.edu/talbot/unbound)
  - Notes: Bible texts online in many languages with parallel viewing.

- [Amharic Bible](https://bible.org/sites/bible.org/resources/foreign/amharic/)
  - Notes: Pretty easy to navigate around, and a useful resource overall.

- [Amharic Book Collection](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401173107/http://www.good-amharic-books.com/library?category=Scripture)
  - Notes: Many different texts, organized by topic.

- [PDF Amharic Bible](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401173107/http://www.biblica.com/bibles/amharic/)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: Old and New Testaments; pretty nice PDF files.

- [Buy the Bible](https://web.archive.org/web/20230401173107/http://www.lojsociety.org/books)
  - Notes: Interlinear translations, plus other books, and plenty of RasTafari stuff

- [Chrestomathia Aethiopica](https://archive.org/details/EthiopicethiopianLanguageGrammarByAugustDillmann)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: A PDF of about 6 MB, contains many Ethiopic texts.

- [Contendings of the Apostles](https://archive.org/details/contendingsofapo00budg)
  - Notes: In Ethiopic with an English translation.

- [Ethiopic Book of Enoch](https://archive.org/details/ethiopicversiono00charuoft)
  - Format: PDF
  - Notes: 14.7 MB PDF file.

- [Liber Henoch](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=DigVAAAAQAAJ&printsec=frontcover&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Seems to be the same as above, only this one is edited by Dillmann, so how could you say no?

- [Royal Chronicle of Abbysinia](https://archive.org/details/royalchronicleof00welduoft)
  - Notes: Not for the faint of heart, either.

- [Ethiopic Poetry](https://archive.org/details/APoesiaEtiopica)
  - Notes: With a translation in Portuguese.

- [History of Minas](https://archive.org/details/historiadosmarty00este)
  - Notes: With a translation in Portuguese as well.

- [History of the Martyrs of Nagran](https://archive.org/details/historiadosmarty00este)
  - Notes: Same as above, with Portuguese translation.

- [Dottrina Cristiana](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=GbEPAAAAIAAJ&pg=PP9&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Includes Arabic and Italian text, plus some very nice hands in the first few scans.

- [Dottrina Cristiana](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=GbEPAAAAIAAJ&pg=PP9&redir_esc=y)
  - Notes: Includes Arabic and Italian text, plus some very nice hands in the first few scans.
